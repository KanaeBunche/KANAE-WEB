Background

Who Am I?

My name is Kanae Bunche, I am first a woman of God who fears the Lord. I am then a sister and a daughter. Then I am a student currently obtaining my Bachelor in Political Science at SUNY Plattsburgh and am pursuing Software Engineering through Flatiron Boot Camp. I was born and raised in the South Bronx, where some people dare to go. I have been faced with challenges in life but never have those challenges prevented me from dreaming and dedicating my life to accomplishing those dreams.

Why I Pursure Software Engineering ?

I am pursuing software engineering because it has become a part of who I am. At first, it was very intimidating, I had to talk to a computer using sentences and phrases, I normally would not form together. But as time went by coding became a challenge my brian yearned for. It was as if I was able to access another part of my brain. I am always learning and growing in this profession which keeps me continuously humbled and ready for the next challenge!!

What are my Aspirations?

I see software engineering as a way to give back to my community and share my skills with those who are unaware of these opportunities or who may not have the resources to pursue software engineering. I want to be able to teach the less fortunate about this aspect of computers to give them hope for a better future, and allow someone else to experience a sense of purpose and meaning in life, while simplifying acquiring a skill.
